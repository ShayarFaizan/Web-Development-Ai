5 AI Video Trends to Watch in 2024

AI is transforming how brands create, edit, and share video content. Here are the top trends shaping the future of video marketing and content creation in 2024.

By Marketing Club • June 2024

The world of video is evolving at lightning speed, and artificial intelligence is at the heart of this revolution. From automated editing to hyper-personalized content, AI is making video creation faster, smarter, and more creative than ever before. Whether you're a marketer, creator, or business owner, understanding these trends will help you stay ahead of the curve and connect with your audience in new ways.

1

AI-Generated Avatars & Hosts

AI avatars are now hosting explainer videos, tutorials, and even brand stories. These digital humans can speak in multiple languages, adapt expressions, and deliver content 24/7—making video creation scalable and global.

Example: Tools like Synthesia and DeepBrain AI let brands create lifelike video presenters from just a script, saving time and money on traditional shoots.

Looking ahead, expect AI avatars to become more interactive, able to answer questions in real time and adapt their style to different audiences.

2

Automated Video Editing

AI-powered editors can now cut, crop, add effects, and even match music to mood—instantly. This means brands can produce more content, faster, and keep up with ever-changing trends on platforms like Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.

Example: Runway and Descript use AI to automate editing, remove backgrounds, and even generate new scenes from text prompts.

In the future, expect AI to suggest creative edits, optimize videos for different platforms, and even A/B test versions for best performance.

3

Voice Synthesis & Dubbing

AI voice tools can now create realistic voiceovers in any language or accent. This is making it easier for brands to localize content and reach new audiences without hiring multiple voice actors.

Example: ElevenLabs and Resemble AI generate natural-sounding voices, while Papercup and Dubverse automate dubbing for global reach.

Soon, AI will enable real-time voice translation and emotional voiceovers that match the mood and context of your video.

4

Personalized Video Content

AI can now tailor videos for each viewer—changing names, offers, or even visuals based on user data. This hyper-personalization boosts engagement and conversion rates dramatically.

Example: Platforms like Idomoo and SundaySky let brands create thousands of personalized video variations for email, ads, and more.

Expect AI-driven personalization to get even smarter, using real-time data to adapt videos on the fly for every viewer.

5

Real-Time Video Analytics

AI-driven analytics tools now provide instant feedback on viewer engagement, sentiment, and drop-off points. Brands can optimize their videos in real time for maximum impact and ROI.

Example: Vidyard and Wistia use AI to track how viewers interact with videos, while Google's Vision AI can analyze video content for trends and safety.

Looking forward, AI analytics will help creators test ideas, predict trends, and make every video smarter and more effective.

Conclusion: The Future of AI Video is Here

AI is no longer just a buzzword in video marketing—it's the engine driving the next wave of creativity, efficiency, and audience connection. By embracing these trends, brands and creators can produce more engaging, effective, and scalable video content than ever before.

Ready to take your video strategy to the next level? Start experimenting with AI tools and stay tuned for even more innovation in the months ahead.
